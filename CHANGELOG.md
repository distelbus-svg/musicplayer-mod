# Changelog

## v1.0.0 — 2026-05-26

-Added Vanilla Minecraft soundtrack support — play Minecraft music directly in the mod
-Added album art system with ID3v2 APIC extraction and soundtrack album art
-Added audio visualizer — live 32-bar frequency bars above the hotbar
-Added mini player — movable, resizable overlay with controls
-Added now-playing HUD and notification system
-Added 5 color themes (Dark, Light, Ocean, Nether, End)
-Added source toggle to switch between user music and Minecraft soundtrack
-Added audio DSP with ducking, reverb, and filters
-Added volume duck on damage
-Added up next / queue system
-Added M3U playlist file support
-Added listening statistics tracker
-Added playlist sorting and checkbox selection for editing
-Added seek support for vanilla Minecraft tracks
-Added pause in single player option
-Added mod icon for Prism Launcher and mod managers
-Added custom volume keybind
-Renamed to Melodix Music Player
-Fixed game freeze on Creator (Music Box) / Comforting Memories tracks
-Fixed search playing the wrong song
-Fixed clicking soundtrack playing a random variant
-Fixed failed-track cascade freeze
-Fixed wrong play/pause button state during transitions
-Fixed fade-on-pause for vanilla tracks
-Fixed environmental music playing while mod plays custom music
-Fixed stop-when-unfocused behavior
-Improved playlist system with sorting and editing
-Improved UI responsiveness and transitions

Coming features in the future:
-YouTube support: Play songs from YouTube directly inside the mod
-Expanding and improving the playlist feature
